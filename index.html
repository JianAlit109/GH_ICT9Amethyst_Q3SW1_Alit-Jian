Spy x Family


Sypnosis:
A spy on an undercover mission gets married and adopts a child as part of his cover. His wife and daughter have secrets of their own. All three must maintain their relationship for their own benefits.

Characters:

Loid Forger
A man acting under the code name "Twilight". He has discarded everything about his past to work for his country as an undercover spy.
Anya Forger
She is also known as Test Subject "007". During her time in an experiment conducted by an unknown organization, she gained abilties that allows her to read minds.
Yor Forger
She leads a secret life as an assassin, wherein she acts under the code name "Thorn Princess". She hides this identity by working as an ordinary clerk at Berlint City Hall.
